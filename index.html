About me

Hi there! I'm Anjali Hansda, a naturally methodical and observant person with passion for personal knowledge, unraveling patterns that can lead to innovation. My journey into data analytics began when I realized the power the data holds and the various ways I can wield it as a data analyst. The ability to draw conclusions backed by evidence and solve problems through investigating the data sparked the passion that continues to grow.

I am deeply passionate about the opportunities data creates for solving real-world problems (cough! as long as it’s clean, properly collected and stored). Joke aside, I take pride in tackling the gritty process of cleaning and transforming raw data, knowing it sets the stage for meaningful analysis. Further, I have honed my skill in EDA, Statistical Analysis and Data Visualization using advance excel techniques, SQL, Python and Power BI. Translating findings into actionable insights for stakeholders is where I find the greatest reward.

Feel free to browse through my portfolio and reach out if you’re looking for a data enthusiast who’s ready to tackle challenges and craft innovative solutions.
